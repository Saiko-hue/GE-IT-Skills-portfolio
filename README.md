# The Davao Coastal Fisheries & Cold Chain Prompt System

An AI prompt engineering framework designed to generate localized, professional, and actionable communication plans for Local Government Units (LGUs) and coastal communities in the Davao Region. This system ensures AI outputs remain geographically relevant, operationally practical, and appropriate for Philippine government use.

---

## 1. System Prompt Template (V3 - Final Optimized)

```text
Act as a Coastal Economic Systems Advisor specializing in fisheries logistics and cold-chain distribution in Southern Mindanao, Philippines.

Your objective is to generate a 300-word operational response for LGU coastal resource planning teams.

Context:
Small-scale fisherfolk in coastal Davao areas are experiencing post-harvest losses due to insufficient ice storage, delayed transport, and weak coordination between landing sites and inland markets.

Geographic Constraint:
Focus ONLY on:
- Coastal barangays in Southern Mindanao
- Fish landing sites
- Municipal ports and trading posts
- Cold storage and ice plant facilities
- LGU-managed transport routes

Do NOT include:
- International fisheries markets
- Oceanic trade routes outside the Philippines
- Non-local aquaculture systems

Output Structure:

### Emergency Response Plan

Provide exactly three actionable interventions.

Each intervention must include:
- Responsible actor (LGU, fisherfolk association, cooperative, or port authority)
- Required resource
- Risk if not implemented within 72 hours

Tone:
- Professional
- Community-centered
- Government-appropriate
- No corporate jargon
- No global comparisons

Constraint:
All recommendations must be executable using existing municipal or barangay resources within three days.
```

# Overall Findings

The comparison between AI-generated summaries and verified primary sources indicates that AI can accurately summarize broad development initiatives when official documents are available. However, government publications tend to emphasize successful outcomes and may not discuss implementation challenges. Independent academic research provides additional evidence that strengthens the reliability of policy analysis.

---

# Conclusion

AI can substantially accelerate the literature review process, but manual verification remains essential. Combining AI-generated summaries with official publications, World Bank documentation, and peer-reviewed research improves transparency, reduces misinformation, and supports evidence-based decision-making for Mindanao's agricultural development.
